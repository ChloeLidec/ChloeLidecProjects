Season 1

The season 1 of Supergirl is about Kara's journey of becoming Supergirl. It's one of the most well written season because it was on CBS and explored Kara's trauma and planned to explore her sexuality and the differences with Krypton

Season 2

Season 2 brought the amazing Lena Luthor but also allowed a sexual harraser to self-insert himself into Mon-el's character. Kara was forgotten as a lead and all the work on her character long time forgotten as well.

Season 3

The season 3 has one if not the most powerful villain of the show. Reign is a complex character and if we forget the attempt at the redemption arc (failed), the season is actually good.

Season 4

Season 4 was once again a good season, it has a complex character (Red Daughter) and explore some deep topics such as Trans identity and racism in today's society.

Season 5

Season 5 is a really bad season as a whole. First the screen-time is occupied by unnecessary men and the character developpement of the women is forgotten. Lex Luthor is once again taking screentime and due to Covid, the season wasn't ended.

Season 6

Season 6 still has major problems especially for the final season. The lead hasn't a well defined storytime and women are once again sidelined for unnecessary men. Then we have the whole Queerbaiting situation that is still going on(now proudly demonstrated by some writers...). The season again has some good points but all in all it shows the network's lack of care for this show...
